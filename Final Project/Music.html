Julie O.


Julie O. aka Julie Outrage is a guitarist, singer-songwritter and percussion player currently residing in Washington DC and Brooklyn, NY. Co-founder of the DMV band The Poets of Unk.
Her style has been called funk-rock with a jazz guitar influence, a feel encompassing hip hop inspired & spoken word lyrics.
Her debut Solo EP "Unknown Artist" is set to be realeased in March 2017. Single for the EP "Untitled" is available on Spotify, iTunes and Amazon Music.
